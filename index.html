About Me

Electrical & Electronics Engineer

I am a senior Electrical and Electronics Engineering student at Middle East Technical University (METU). My professional interest lies at the intersection of hardware and software, specifically focusing on ASIC/SoC Design, FPGA prototyping, and Digital Verification.

I have a hands-on background in developing hardware modules using Verilog and SystemVerilog, and I am experienced in modern verification frameworks like UVM and Cocotb. From implementing asynchronous FIFO designs to working on specialized projects like the Sparse-V-Accelerator, I am driven by the challenge of designing efficient, high-performance digital systems.

I am currently seeking opportunities to contribute to cutting-edge semiconductor technologies and digital design teams globally.
